What I Do

Product Discovery and Strategy:

  • Conduct comprehensive market research, competitor analysis, and user persona development to identify key problems and opportunities.

  • Conduct user interviews and surveys to uncover pain points and validate product concepts.

  • Develop and manage product roadmaps, ensuring timely delivery of features and enhancements.

  • Collaborate with engineering, design, and marketing teams to ensure successful product development and implementation.
